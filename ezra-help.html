🚀 Getting Started with Ezra

Opening Ezra

Look for the gold orb (✦) floating in the corner of your screen. Click it to open Ezra. You can drag the orb to reposition it anywhere.

💡 Pro Tip: Double-click the orb for quick open/close. Press Ctrl/Cmd + E as a keyboard shortcut.

Your First Quote

New to Ezra? Click the 🚀 Quick Quote button for a guided walkthrough, or simply type your deal details naturally:

"$800k home, $400k mortgage, need $100k cash for John Smith"

💬 Natural Language Commands

Type naturally - Ezra understands context:

Change cash to $150K Updates HELOC amount

🎤 Voice Commands Platinum

🎙️ To activate: Click the microphone button in the input area or press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + V

Creating Quotes by Voice

Simply speak naturally. Ezra understands:

"Create a quote for John Smith"
"$800,000 property, $400,000 mortgage, needs $100,000 cash"
"Client is Michael Brown, 750 credit score, $600,000 home value"

Voice Shortcuts

Say This Result
"Go with tier 1" Selects highest origination fee tier
"Switch to tier 2" Selects middle tier
"Use 15-year fixed" Selects 15-year fixed program
"Change to 30-year" Switches to 30-year term
"Make it variable" Switches to variable rate program
"Undo" Reverts last action
"Clear chat" Clears conversation history

Rate Input by Voice

"For the 4.99 origination fee, the 30-year is 7.45%, 20-year is 7.20%"
"At 2.99 origination: 30 years at 7.85%, 20 years at 7.60%"
"The 1.50 origination fee: 30-year rate is 8.35%"

🎯 Example Workflows

Quick Quote

  1. Click 🚀 Quick Quote
  2. Answer 4 questions
  3. Get complete quote
  4. Click "Generate Link"

Voice Quote

  1. Click 🎤 microphone
  2. Say: "$800K property, $400K mortgage, need $100K cash"
  3. Confirm details
  4. Say: "Go with tier 2, 15-year fixed"

Portal Import

  1. Open Figure portal
  2. Press Ctrl+A, then Ctrl+C
  3. Click 📋 Paste Rates button
  4. Ctrl+V, click Import
  5. All fields auto-filled!
